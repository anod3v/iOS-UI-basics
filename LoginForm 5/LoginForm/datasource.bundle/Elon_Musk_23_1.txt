Musk's Tesla Roadster in orbit after the Falcon Heavy test flight in 2018
On February 6, 2018, SpaceX successfully launched the Falcon Heavy, the fourth-highest capacity rocket ever built (after Saturn V, Energia and N1) and the most powerful rocket in operation as of 2018.[citation needed] The inaugural mission carried a Tesla Roadster belonging to Musk as a dummy payload.[100][101]
