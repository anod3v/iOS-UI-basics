In May 2002, Musk founded SpaceX, an aerospace manufacturer and space transport services company, of which he is CEO and lead designer. He joined Tesla Motors, Inc. (now Tesla, Inc.), an electric vehicle manufacturer, in 2004, the year after it was founded,[10] became its product architect, and became its CEO in October 2008.[26] (Musk says "Chief Engineer" of both SpaceX and Tesla would be a more accurate title than CEO.)[27] In 2006, he helped create SolarCity, a solar energy services company (now a subsidiary of Tesla).
